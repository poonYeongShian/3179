Sustainable Transport: CO2 Emissions & EV Sales

This data was sourced from the website 'Our World in Data,' curated by Hannah Ritchie and Max Roser, which tracks CO2 emissions. It calculates the individual contribution of each country's average citizen by dividing the country's total emissions by its population. This calculation provides us with the metric of CO2 emissions per capita. The visualization demonstrates the disparities in per capita emissions worldwide.

In this dataset of global CO2 emissions per capita,Qatar tops the list with 35.59 tons of CO2 emissions per capita, while the Democratic Republic of Congo has the lowest at 0.0272 tons, revealing significant global disparities.
